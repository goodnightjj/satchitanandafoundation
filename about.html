Mission

We are manifesting an awakened present and abundant future for all conscious beings.

Outcomes

End global poverty and hunger

Specifically by 2050 zero human beings on the planet lack access to sufficient food, water or shelter.

Spread freedom across the globe

Specifically by 2050 US governance returns to its founding principles of protecting our inalienable rights to life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness and inspires countries across the developing world to adopt the same.

Foster flourishing and spiritual awakening
